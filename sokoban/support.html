Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control the game?
On iPhone and iPad: swipe anywhere on the board in the direction you want to move, or tap the D-pad buttons at the bottom. On Apple Watch: swipe directly on the board.
Can I undo a move?
Yes — tap the ↩ undo button to go back one step. You can undo as many times as you like, all the way back to the start of the level.
Why did a crate turn red?
A red crate means it has been pushed into a corner or position from which it can never reach a target — the level is now unsolvable from this state. Tap undo to reverse the move.
I bought the full game on iPhone — do I need to pay again on Apple Watch?
No. The purchase is linked to your Apple ID. Tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall screen on any device and it will unlock immediately at no extra charge.
How do I restore a purchase?
Tap any locked level (Level 16+) to open the unlock screen, then tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.
The Apple Watch app says "will connect on demand" — is that normal?
Yes, that's normal. The Watch app runs independently and connects wirelessly when needed. Make sure your Watch is unlocked and your iPhone is nearby with Bluetooth on.
Does the game collect any of my data?
No. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored anywhere other than your own device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
I'm stuck on a level — any hints?
Every level is solvable — verified by an A* algorithm solver. Use unlimited undo to experiment. General tip: plan where each crate needs to go before pushing, and think about which crate to move first. Avoid pushing crates into corners unless that corner has a target.
