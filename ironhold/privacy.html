Privacy Policy

Ironhold · 鐵壁雄關

✓ No personal data collected

Overview

Ironhold is developed by Lynncat (lynncat.com). We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

What We Collect

Nothing. Specifically:

  • No account or registration required
  • No name, email, or contact information collected
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising networks

Local Storage

Game progress, best waves, upgrades and your local leaderboard name are saved on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults API. This data never leaves your device.

Game Center

Global leaderboards are optional. If you sign in to Game Center, your score and Game Center alias are handled by Apple, not by us. Apple's Privacy Policy applies: apple.com/privacy. The game works fully offline if you choose not to use Game Center.

No In-App Purchases

Ironhold is completely free with no in-app purchases and no advertising.

Children

Ironhold contains only mild, abstract strategy combat and is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone.

✓ 不收集任何個人資料

概述

鐵壁雄關由 Lynncat（lynncat.com）開發。我們承諾保護您的隱私。本政策說明我們的資料處理方式。

資料收集

我們不收集任何資料。具體包括：

  • 無需註冊帳號
  • 不收集姓名、電子郵件或任何聯絡方式
  • 不收集位置資訊
  • 不收集裝置識別碼
  • 不使用任何分析或追蹤 SDK
  • 不接入任何廣告網路

本機儲存

遊戲進度、最佳波數、強化與本機排行榜名號透過 Apple UserDefaults 儲存於裝置本機，不會上傳至任何伺服器。

Game Center

全球排行榜為可選功能。若您登入 Game Center，您的分數與 Game Center 暱稱由 Apple 處理，而非由我們處理。適用 Apple 隱私權政策：apple.com/tw/privacy。若不使用 Game Center，遊戲仍可完全離線進行。

無應用程式內購

鐵壁雄關完全免費，無內購、無廣告。

兒童

鐵壁雄關僅含輕度、抽象的策略對戰內容，適合一般大眾。我們不會在知情情況下收集任何人的資訊。

聯絡方式

如有問題，請發送電子郵件至 amwpessy@gmail.com
