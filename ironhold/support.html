Ironhold · 鐵壁雄關

What is Ironhold?

An ink-painted strategy tower defense. You defend a city wall against endless escalating waves by deploying defenders across several lanes.

How do I play?

Recruit defenders into lanes using the resources you earn. Hold off each wave; survive as long as you can. A powerful boss arrives every five waves.

Is there any strategy?

Yes. Pick one of three difficulties (Cadet / General / Commander). Shields blunt arrows, so bring melee or catapults to break them. Spend merit to upgrade bows, blades and armor, and trigger the cooldown ability Arrow Rain in a crisis.

How do leaderboards work?

Each difficulty has its own ranking. Scores sync to Game Center global leaderboards when signed in, and there is also a fully offline on-device board.

Is it free?

Completely free — no ads and no in-app purchases.

Does it work on Apple Watch?

Yes. Apple Watch runs a self-contained three-lane version — no nearby iPhone required.

How do I change the language?

The app follows your device language, with full support for English, Simplified Chinese and Traditional Chinese.

鐵壁雄關是什麼？

一款水墨風格的策略塔防。你要在多條車道上部署守軍,抵禦一波波漸強的大軍,守住城牆。

怎麼遊玩？

用累積的資源在各車道招募守軍,擋下每一波攻勢,盡可能存活更久。每五波會迎戰一位強力首領。

有哪些策略玩法？

開局可選三檔難度(校尉 / 將軍 / 統帥)。盾兵會擋箭,需用近戰或投石破之;用戰功升級強弓、利刃、固防,並在危急時釋放冷卻技能「箭雨」。

排行榜如何運作？

每個難度各有排名。登入 Game Center 時分數會同步至全球排行榜,另有完全離線的本機榜。

是免費的嗎？

完全免費——無廣告、無內購。

支援 Apple Watch 嗎？

支援。Apple Watch 上有獨立的三車道版本,毋須 iPhone 在旁。

如何切換語言？

App 會跟隨你的裝置語言,完整支援英文、簡體中文與繁體中文。

還需要協助？

請來信 amwpessy@gmail.com,我們通常會在數日內回覆。
