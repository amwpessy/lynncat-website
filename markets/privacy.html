Overview

Lynncat Markets is developed by Lynncat (lynncat.com). This policy explains how the app handles privacy across the iPhone, Apple Watch and Mac versions.

What We Collect

We do not require an account and we do not collect information that directly identifies you. Specifically:

  • No account or registration required
  • No name, email address, phone number, contacts or address book data collected
  • No precise or approximate location data collected
  • No advertising identifier collected
  • No third-party analytics, advertising or tracking SDKs
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking

Local Storage

The app saves preferences on your device using Apple's standard local storage APIs, including selected region/language, exchange-rate display settings, watchlist state, price alert settings, theme choices and similar app preferences. This information stays on your device and is not sent to Lynncat.

Price Alerts and Notifications

If you enable price alerts, alert rules are stored locally on your device. Price checks and notification scheduling are handled by the app and Apple system notification services. Lynncat does not receive your alert rules.

Market, News and Version Requests

To provide the app's core features, Lynncat Markets makes network requests to public or third-party data sources for market prices, exchange rates, historical charts, economic events and news feeds. The app may also request a Lynncat-hosted configuration file to check whether a minimum supported version is required.

These requests are used to load app content and generally include only technical request information and market/news identifiers, such as asset symbols or feed URLs. Data handling by third-party data providers is governed by their own privacy policies.

Messages and User-Generated Content

The current App Store version has the public message board feature disabled. The app does not provide a public posting or chat feature in this release.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information for advertising or tracking purposes.

Children

The app is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Financial Disclaimer

Market prices, news and economic information are provided for reference only and do not constitute investment, financial, trading or legal advice. You are responsible for your own decisions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when app features or legal requirements change. The updated date below indicates the latest revision.
