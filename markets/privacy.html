Overview

Lynncat Markets is developed by Lynncat (lynncat.com). This policy explains how the app handles privacy across the iPhone, Apple Watch and Mac versions.

Optional Lynncat Account

You can browse market data, news and public discussions without an account. Sign in with Apple is required only to earn or spend Lynncat Points, publish discussion messages and use account features. Lynncat receives an opaque Apple user identifier and authentication credentials from Apple; we do not request your Apple ID password, contacts, phone number or address book.

We store a one-way hash of the Apple user identifier, an encrypted Apple refresh token used for account management and deletion, a public Lynncat account ID, your generated or chosen nickname, and session records. These records link your account across the iPhone, Apple Watch and Mac versions.

What We Collect

When you use the optional account and community features, we process:

  • User ID: the pseudonymous account identifiers described above
  • Device ID: a salted hash of an app-generated installation identifier and the device platform
  • User content: nickname, discussion text, selected market room, reports and optional report notes
  • Product interaction: foreground heartbeat times, points balance, points ledger and leaderboard visibility
  • No email address, phone number, contacts or address book data collected by Lynncat
  • No precise or approximate location data collected
  • No advertising identifier collected
  • No third-party analytics, advertising or tracking SDKs
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking

Local Storage

The app saves preferences on your device using Apple's standard local storage APIs, including selected region/language, exchange-rate display settings, watchlist state, price alert settings, theme choices and similar app preferences. Account session tokens are stored in the Apple Keychain. Local preferences stay on your device unless a feature description above says that data is sent to Lynncat.

Price Alerts and Notifications

If you enable price alerts, alert rules are stored locally on your device. Price checks and notification scheduling are handled by the app and Apple system notification services. Lynncat does not receive your alert rules.

Market, News and Version Requests

To provide the app's core features, Lynncat Markets makes network requests to public or third-party data sources for market prices, exchange rates, historical charts, economic events and news feeds. The app may also request a Lynncat-hosted configuration file to check whether a minimum supported version is required.

These requests are used to load app content and generally include only technical request information and market/news identifiers, such as asset symbols or feed URLs. Data handling by third-party data providers is governed by their own privacy policies.

Lynncat Points and Leaderboard

While a signed-in app is visible in the foreground, it sends periodic heartbeats with the app platform and installation identifier. The server uses these interactions to award one Lynncat Point for each eligible foreground minute on each active device. It stores credit and debit entries, resulting balances and timestamps to prevent duplicate awards and support account history.

Publishing a message costs three Lynncat Points. Points are an in-app participation unit only: they have no cash value, cannot be purchased, transferred, redeemed or exchanged, and are not a financial asset.

The leaderboard is visible to app users. If leaderboard visibility is enabled, only your nickname, points balance and rank are public; your Apple identifier, installation identifier and ledger are not public. You can hide or show your entry in account settings.

Messages and User-Generated Content

Market-specific discussion tickers allow anyone to read public messages, while publishing requires a signed-in Lynncat account. When you participate, Lynncat processes your nickname, submitted text, selected market room, posting and expiry timestamps, linked account ID and an opaque author key.

The service uses pseudonymous account and installation identifiers for cooldown enforcement, point charging and abuse prevention. If a post is reported, the report reason, optional note, message status and necessary moderation history are also stored. Apple identifiers, session credentials, installation hashes, moderator credentials and internal audit-only data are not returned to ordinary app users.

Posts expire from public display one hour after publication. Server records may be retained only as needed for abuse prevention, reports, legal compliance and moderation integrity. Local author blocks remain on your device and are not uploaded as a social graph.

This feature does not access your contacts, precise location, advertising identifier or data from other apps, and it is not used for advertising or cross-app tracking. See the Community Rules for reporting and blocking details.

Retention and Account Deletion

Account, device, session, heartbeat lease, points ledger and profile records are kept while your Lynncat account is active. To remove them, open account settings and choose Delete Lynncat Account. The service asks Apple to revoke the account credential, then deletes the account records and active messages linked to that account. A temporary error may require you to retry so deletion is not reported as complete before Apple revocation succeeds.

Posts expire from public display one hour after publication. Reported or moderated records may be retained only as needed for abuse prevention, legal compliance and moderation integrity. Local author blocks remain on your device and are not uploaded as a social graph.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information or share it for advertising or tracking. Apple processes Sign in with Apple, and our hosting provider processes the account and community data only to operate, secure and maintain these features.

Children

The app is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Financial Disclaimer

Market prices, news and economic information are provided for reference only and do not constitute investment, financial, trading or legal advice. You are responsible for your own decisions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy when app features or legal requirements change. The updated date below indicates the latest revision.

  • 不收集廣告識別碼
  • 不使用第三方分析、廣告或追蹤 SDK
  • 不進行跨 App 或跨網站追蹤

App 會透過 Apple 標準本機儲存 API，在您的裝置上保存偏好設定，包括所選地區/語言、匯率顯示設定、自選狀態、價格提醒設定、主題選擇及類似 App 偏好。帳號工作階段權杖保存在 Apple 鑰匙圈。本機偏好會留在裝置上，除非上述功能說明明確指出資料會傳送給 Lynncat。

這些請求用於載入 App 內容，通常僅包含技術性請求資訊及行情/資訊識別內容，例如資產代碼或 RSS 來源網址。第三方資料提供者如何處理資料，受其各自隱私政策約束。

Lynncat 積分與排行榜

已登入的 App 在前景顯示時，會定期傳送包含 App 平台及安裝識別碼的在線心跳。伺服器會依這些互動，為每台符合條件的前景裝置每分鐘增加一個 Lynncat 積分，並保存積分增加、扣除、結果餘額與時間，以防重複計算並提供帳號明細。

每次發佈交流內容會扣除三個 Lynncat 積分。積分只是在 App 內使用的參與單位，沒有現金價值，不可購買、轉讓、兌現或交換，也不是金融資產。

排行榜可由 App 使用者查看。啟用排行榜顯示時，只有您的暱稱、積分餘額及名次會公開；Apple 識別碼、安裝識別碼和積分明細不會公開。您可在帳號設定中隱藏或顯示自己的排名。

交流與使用者產生內容

所有人都可閱讀分品種市場交流滾動內容，但發佈內容需要已登入的 Lynncat 帳號。當您參與交流時，Lynncat 會處理您的暱稱、提交文字、所選市場房間、發佈與到期時間、關聯帳號識別碼及不透明作者識別碼。
