Privacy Policy

Lynncat Markets · 靈貓行情

✓ No personal data collected

Overview

Lynncat Markets is developed by Lynncat (lynncat.com). We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

What We Collect

Nothing. Specifically:

  • No account or registration required
  • No name, email, or contact information collected
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No advertising networks

Local Storage

Your preferences — selected region, exchange rate and price alerts — are saved on your device using Apple's standard UserDefaults API. This data never leaves your device and we cannot access it.

Network Requests

To show live quotes, the app makes read-only requests to third-party public APIs: gold-api.com (metal & crypto prices), open.er-api.com (exchange rates), Sina Finance (metal history) and Binance / CoinGecko (crypto history). These requests contain only the necessary asset symbols and no personal information. Data handling by these services is governed by their own privacy policies.

Notifications

If you enable price alerts, all calculations and notifications happen locally on your device. No server is involved.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences and collects no data from anyone.

Disclaimer

Prices are provided for reference only and do not constitute investment advice.

您的偏好設定——所選地區、匯率與價格提醒——透過 Apple UserDefaults 儲存於裝置本機,不會離開您的裝置,我們亦無法存取。

為顯示即時行情,App 會向第三方公開介面發出唯讀請求:gold-api.com（貴金屬與加密價格）、open.er-api.com（匯率）、新浪財經（貴金屬歷史）及 Binance / CoinGecko（加密歷史）。這些請求僅包含必要的資產代碼,不含任何個人資料。各介面的資料處理受其各自隱私政策約束。

通知

若您啟用價格提醒,所有運算與通知均在您的裝置本機完成,不涉及伺服器。
