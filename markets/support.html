Lynncat Markets · 靈貓行情

What is Lynncat Markets?

A clean, real-time price tracker for precious metals (gold, silver, platinum, palladium) and crypto (Bitcoin, Ethereum), for iPhone, iPad, Mac and Apple Watch.

How do I switch currency?

Tap the region selector to switch between CNY, HKD and USD. Metals are priced per gram and crypto per coin; USD mode also switches the interface to English.

How do price alerts work?

Set a target price and choose above or below. When the price crosses it, you get a local notification. Alerts can be set in CNY or USD.

Where does the data come from?

Prices come from third-party public data sources and are for reference only — they do not constitute investment advice.

Does it work on Mac and Apple Watch?

Yes. On Mac it lives in your menu bar (with Touch Bar support); on Apple Watch it's a standalone app you can glance at without an iPhone nearby.

Is it free?

Completely free — no ads, no tracking, and no personal data collected.

How do I change the language?

The app follows your device language, with full support for English, Simplified Chinese and Traditional Chinese.

靈貓行情是什麼？

一款簡潔的即時行情工具,涵蓋貴金屬(黃金、白銀、鉑金、鈀金)與加密貨幣(比特幣、以太幣),支援 iPhone、iPad、Mac 與 Apple Watch。

如何切換貨幣？

點擊地區選擇器即可在人民幣、港幣、美元之間切換。貴金屬按克、加密按枚計價;美元模式同時將介面切換為英文。

價格提醒如何運作？

設定目標價並選擇突破上限或下限,當價格越過時即收到本機通知。提醒可用人民幣或美元設定。

數據來自哪裡？

行情來自第三方公開數據來源,僅供參考——不構成任何投資建議。

支援 Mac 與 Apple Watch 嗎？

支援。Mac 上常駐選單列(並支援 Touch Bar);Apple Watch 上為獨立 App,毋須 iPhone 在旁即可查看。

是免費的嗎？

完全免費——無廣告、不追蹤,亦不收集任何個人資料。

如何切換語言？

App 會跟隨你的裝置語言,完整支援英文、簡體中文與繁體中文。

還需要協助？

請來信 amwpessy@gmail.com,我們通常會在數日內回覆。
