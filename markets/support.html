Lynncat Markets · 靈貓行情

What can I track in Lynncat Markets?

Precious metals, Bitcoin, Ethereum, US stock indexes, selected US stocks, US Treasury data, crude oil, recent economic events, technology news and current news.

What is included in the gold page?

Live gold pricing, historical charts, China central bank monthly gold reserve changes, US gold ETF daily holding changes, ratio analysis, and Gold ⇄ Bitcoin conversion.

Does the app support US market data?

Yes. The US stocks page includes Dow Jones, Nasdaq and S&P 500 panels, index history charts, stock prices, daily changes and market value information. Compact external displays use Nasdaq data.

Do news and economic events update?

The economic events, technology news and current-news pages are designed to refresh from live sources when opened or when you tap update. Speed depends on your network and the source service.

Can I switch language or region?

Yes. The app supports English, Simplified Chinese and Traditional Chinese. News pages use bilingual sources so content can switch with the app language where available.

How do price alerts work?

Price alert rules are saved locally on your device. Lynncat does not receive your alert levels. Delivery depends on system notification permissions and background refresh behavior.

Does Lynncat Markets support Lock Screen and Dynamic Island?

On compatible iPhone models, market information can appear through Lock Screen Live Activities and Dynamic Island. You can enable or stop this display from the app.

What does the Mac version include?

The Mac app includes the full market dashboard, menu bar quotes, compact chart displays, optional light theme, price alerts and a standby-style gold trend screen.

How does market discussion work?

Each concrete market symbol or standalone page has an independent discussion ticker. Tap a post to Report it or block that author locally. Posts expire from public display after one hour, and Mac and iPhone users can post subject to a three-minute room cooldown. Read the Community Rules before posting.

Still need help?

Email amwpessy@gmail.com with your device model, system version, app version and a short description of the issue.

這個 App 可以追蹤哪些內容？

你可以查看貴金屬、比特幣、以太坊、美股指數、部分美股、美国国债數據、原油價格、近期重要經濟事件、科技資訊與時事資訊。

黃金頁面包含哪些功能？

黃金頁面包含即時價格、歷年走势图、中國央行每月黃金儲備增減、美國黃金 ETF 每日持倉增減、比值分析，以及黃金和比特幣換算。

是否支援美股數據？

支援。美股頁面包含道瓊斯、納斯達克、標普 500 指數、指數歷年走势图、美股價格和漲跌幅列表，以及總市值等資訊。外部緊湊顯示使用納斯達克數據。

資訊和經濟事件會更新嗎？

近期重要經濟事件、科技資訊和時事資訊頁面會在打開或點擊更新時從即時來源獲取新內容。實際速度取決於網路和資訊源服務。

能否切換語言和地區？

可以。App 支援英文、簡體中文與繁體中文。資訊類頁面會使用中英雙語來源，盡量隨 App 語言同步切換。

價格提醒如何運作？

價格提醒規則保存在你的裝置本機，Lynncat 不會接收你的提醒價格。提醒是否送達取決於系統通知權限和背景刷新狀態。

是否支援鎖屏和靈動島？

在支援的 iPhone 型號上，App 可以透過即時活動在鎖屏和靈動島顯示市場資訊。你可以在 App 內啟用或停止。

Mac 版本包含哪些功能？

Mac 版本包含完整市場看板、選單列行情、緊湊走势图、可選淺色主題、價格提醒，以及待機式黃金走勢大屏。

市場交流如何運作？

每個具體行情品種或獨立頁面都有自己的交流滾動條，房間之間互不混合。點擊內容可舉報或在本機屏蔽作者；內容公開一小時後過期。Mac 與 iPhone 可發言，同一房間需等待三分鐘才能再次發送。發言前請閱讀《社區規則》。
