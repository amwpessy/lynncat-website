社区规则 · Community Rules

适用于 Lynncat Markets 各行情品种的滚动交流内容。

These rules apply to the market-specific discussion tickers in Lynncat Markets.

简体中文 English

简体中文

分品种交流

每个具体行情品种或独立内容页都有自己的交流房间。黄金、白银、比特币、美股、原油、经济事件和资讯等房间彼此独立，内容不会跨房间混合。

公开时间与身份

无需注册账号。你可以填写公开昵称；昵称和内容会显示在当前房间的滚动条中，并在发布一小时后从公开区域过期。系统使用不透明的访客标识防止滥用，不会向其他用户公开该标识。

禁止内容

  • 辱骂、仇恨、歧视、骚扰、霸凌、暴力威胁或露骨色情内容；
  • 电话号码、社交账号、私人联系方式、个人身份信息或外部导流链接；
  • 诈骗、钓鱼、诱导转账、保证收益、带单跟单、买卖喊单或其他违规金融引导；
  • 重复刷屏、冒充他人，或规避发送冷却与审核措施。

举报与屏蔽

轻触或点击一条交流内容即可举报或在本机屏蔽作者。举报会进入审核流程；达到风险阈值的内容会先从公开滚动条隐藏。屏蔽只在你的设备上生效，可在设置中解除。

市场交流仅供一般信息分享，不构成投资、交易、金融或法律建议。请自行判断风险，不要仅凭其他用户的内容作出交易决定。

English

Market-specific rooms

Each concrete market symbol or standalone content page has its own room. Gold, silver, Bitcoin, US stocks, crude oil, economic events, and news discussions remain separate and are never mixed across rooms.

Public lifetime and identity

No account is required. You may choose a public nickname. Your nickname and post appear in the current room ticker and expire from public display one hour after posting. An opaque guest identifier is used to prevent abuse and is not shown to other users.

Prohibited content

  • Abuse, hate, discrimination, harassment, bullying, violent threats, or explicit sexual content;
  • Phone numbers, social handles, private contact details, personal information, or links that move users off-platform;
  • Scams, phishing, transfer requests, guaranteed returns, copy-trading solicitations, trade calls, or unlawful financial promotion;
  • Repeated spam, impersonation, or attempts to evade cooldown and moderation controls.

Reporting and blocking

Tap or click a post to report it or block its author locally. Reports enter the moderation queue, and content that reaches a risk threshold is hidden from public tickers pending review. Local blocks affect only your device and can be removed in Settings.

Market discussions are general information only and are not investment, trading, financial, or legal advice. Make your own risk assessment and do not trade solely on another user's post.
